Articles

Castor in the press...

IBM developerWorks (2007/2008)

Brett McLaughlin has published an article series on (XML) data binding with Castor at IBM's developerWorks.

-Part 1: Install and set up Castor
-Part 2: Marshall and unmarshall XML
-Part 3: Map between schemas
-Part 4: Bind your Java objects to SQL databases

IBM developerWorks (October 2003)

Elias Torres, Brian D Goodman and Kevin Gibbs explain in their article how to integrate Castor with Apache Axis to process Document Style Web Services. Read

Castor Cures Your Data Binding Ills (developer.com, March 2003)

Thomas Hammell discusses both Castor XML and Castor J/DO as he walks the reader through a real world XML and RDB data binding example. Read

IBM developerWorks (January 2003)

Dennis Sonoski concludes its data-binding serie on developerWorks by taking a look at the performance of the different data-binding frameworks. Read

IBM developerWorks (January 2003)

Dennis Sonoski continues his series on the data binding approach by giving a global overview of 5 data binding frameworks including Castor and JAXB. Read

O/R Mapping with Castor JDO in the Real World (December 2002)

David Colwell discusses the pros and cons of his experience with Castor JDO as well as some feeback about how he worked around some of its limitations. Read

O'Reilly ONJava (October 2002)

Jeff Lowery provides an introduction to the Castor JDO framework. Read

IBM developerWorks (August 2002)

Castor JDO lead Bruce Snyder gives an overview of Castor JDO and introduces the reader to the basics of working with the JDO framework. Read

O'Reilly XML.com (July 2002)

Niel Bornstein drafts a introduction to the .Net Data-Binding framework and to Castor. This introduction compares both approaches. Read

IBM developerWorks (April 2002)

Enterprise Java expert Dennis Sosnoski introduces data binding and discusses what makes it so appealing. He illustrates the article using Castor. Read

Javaworld (April 2002)

Jacek Kruszelnicki discusses the pros and cons of Sun JDO and Castor JDO. Read

Javaworld (December 2001)

Sam Brodkin shed light on the Data-binding world using JAXB and Castor XML. Read

O'Reilly Onjava.com (October 2001)

Dion Almaer gives a good introduction to Castor XML. Read

Atlanta Java Software Symposium (November 2002) - New

XML One Amsterdam (September 2001)

Andrew Fawcett, of CODA - our main evangelist, gave a presentation on implementing XML data binding solutions using web-services with Castor at the XML One conference in Amsterdam, September 2001. Download

O'Reilly Java Conference (March 2000)
